About me

Howdy! My name is Yile Chen (Spoken as “Yi-Le”, pronounced “Yee-La”) and you can call me Allen. The characters “Yi” and “Le” mean “one” and “happy” in Chinese, meaning happy for a lifetime. I believe I have lived up to my name of being a happy guy and I like to bring happiness to others around me. Since the age 5, I lived and studied in the US (Minnesota and Kentucky) and Sweden with my family, until returning to China at the age of 10. My childhood life of living abroad changed my view of the world, as it made me interested in different cultures and willing to have open conversations with those whose ethnicities or social backgrounds are different from mine. It also made me want to pursue my dream of going to college in the US.

Over the course of my life I have developed many hobbies. I played violin since third grade and was in the Philharmonic Orchestra at Texas A&M last year, where I enjoyed performing to the public. In my spare time I like to play the guitar and sing some of my favorite songs while playing. Other hobbies of mine include running and playing basketball. I also enjoy cooking, especially making Chinese food. The process of cooking really intrigues me, from the excitement of learning a new dish, to the feeling of accomplishment after completing it, and finally enjoying the sensation of eating delicious food. Some of my "signiture" dishes include Mapo Tofu, Red-braised pork belly, Cola Chicken, and laziji .

Another major hobby of mine is photography, which I picked up in college. During my study abroad in Hong Kong in summer 2019, I had an unforgettable time as there were so many scenic spots and it really inspired me to take photos. I also made trips to other cities in China like Beijing and Guangzhou during last winter break where I got many satisfying shots. This summer I started getting into astrophotography since there are some great stargazing places in Texas. My dream is to road trip the US, especially visit the national parks, and use my camera to capture the wonders of nature.
